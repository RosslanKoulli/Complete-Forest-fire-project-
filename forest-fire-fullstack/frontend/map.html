Map - region prediction and fire spread

Draw a rectangle on the map to select a region. Hexagons will be coloured by predicted fire probability. Click a hexagon to ignite a fire and watch it spread.

How to use
  1. Use the rectangle tool (top-left of the map) to draw a region.
  2. Wait while hexagons are coloured by predicted fire probability.
  3. Click any hexagon to ignite a fire there.
  4. Watch the fire spread across neighbouring hexagons.
Model

Pick which classifier drives the hex colours and simulation. Average combines all three. Region context (Portugal / Algeria) is auto-detected from each hex's location and does not need to be set manually.

Hex resolution
7

Higher resolution means smaller, more numerous hexes. Resolution 7 gives hexes about 5 km across; resolution 8 about 1.5 km. Larger rectangles at high resolution can hit the 500-hex cap.

Time window
5days

Each simulation step represents 12 real hours. The default start date is 5 days ago because Open-Meteo's archive lags by a few days; you can also pick dates up to 7 days in the future to run against the forecast.
